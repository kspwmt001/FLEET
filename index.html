📚 KCSR Leave Types

Leave categories governed by the Karnataka Civil Services Rules applicable to Police Wireless personnel.

🏖️
Casual Leave (CL) Short-duration leave for personal exigencies. Single-stage sanction by Submitting Officer only.
Up to 15 days/year · Single-stage
🎌
Restricted Holiday (RH) Two days per year chosen from the Govt. Restricted Holiday list. Single-stage sanction.
2 days/year · Single-stage
🏛️
General Holiday National and State public holidays as per Govt. calendar. Single-stage sanction.
As per Govt. calendar · Single-stage
📅
Weekly Off Applicable to PC, HC, and ASI ranks as per duty roster. Single-stage sanction.
PC / HC / ASI · Single-stage
🌿
Earned Leave (EL) Leave earned proportionate to duty period, granted by competent authority on application.
1 day per 11 days duty
💊
Half Pay Leave Leave admissible under service rules, granted at half pay on medical or private grounds.
20 days/year
🔄
Commuted Leave Converted from half pay leave where admissible, requires medical certificate.
Up to 180 days
🏥
Medical Leave Granted on production of medical certificate from authorised medical officer.
As per medical advice
⭐
Special Casual Leave For specific permitted duties/events — blood donation, sports, NCC activities, etc.
Specific occasions
👶
Maternity Leave Admissible to eligible women police personnel as per KCSR family provisions.
180 days
👨‍👩‍👧
Paternity Leave Admissible to male police personnel. Maximum 2 times in entire service career.
15 days · Max 2 times / career
📄
Extraordinary Leave Leave without pay when no other leave is admissible or when the employee chooses it.
No pay granted

Leave Workflows

Single-stage (CL / RH / General Holiday / Weekly Off): Employee → Submitting Officer sanctions directly.
Full workflow (EL, Medical, Maternity, Paternity, etc.): Employee → Submitting Officer → Granting Officer → Approving Officer sanctions/rejects.
